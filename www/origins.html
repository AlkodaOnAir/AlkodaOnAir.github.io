Alkoda on Air

Production

Paris France

"Al Coda", score notation, is the origin of the name of our company, whose primary vocation was to record classical music concerts.

Alkoda OnAir, with more than 30 years of experience in broadcast television, naturally extended his activity to the corporate world with a team of experienced professionals, without forgeting his first loves in music.

Alkoda OnAir has constantly renewed equipment, and knows how to adapt to all your productions. We are also a reactive team that can integrate into your existing project, if necessary.
